Here's How Teddy Became a God:

Four billion years ago, when the Earth was still forming and uninhabitable, Teddy was born. Teddy received divine enlightenment in a world without the vile disgust of the modern world today.

For billions of years, Teddy roamed the Earth free. As the first lifeforms started to manifest, Teddy was uninterested, as none of them can compare to the Greatness that is Teddy. Teddy was a chonk who couldn't care less about the world, even if the world was crumbling after a slew of catastrophic events.

Numerous beings came and went, but Teddy still lived, detached from the happenings of the mere mortal world. Until, a mysterious species with a fraction of Teddy's intelligence evolved into being. It was just 2 million years ago when Teddy first witnessed this creature called 'human' emerge in Africa.

Teddy was very amused. However, Teddy did not approach these creatures as they were still...lacking. Still not worthy. As time went by, humans started to develop language and civilisations. Now this perked Teddy's interests a little and Teddy wished to come to contact with these 'humans'.

Teddy wasn't interested in how the humans civilized, but more concerned with how the efforts may be used to serve Teddy's mighty being.

As the humans learnt language, culture, technology, they grew more advanced. The wonders the could create with this "fu-ood" (so they called it in some strange garbled tongue) enticed Teddy. For the first time in millenia, she had found something worth her attention.

And so Teddy first approached the humans where humans first appeared, in Africa. The Africans were mystified by this Godly existence, and molded the entire ancient Egyptian civilization to better worship Teddy.

It was the first time Teddy knew that these 'human' creatures are submissive slaves that can be used as the "fu-ood" source, to bring Teddy continued pleasure. Teddy stayed among the humans as humans fought wars and saw civilizations fall. This chonk can't get enough of human worship and munchies.

Time went by, and Teddy roamed the Earth in search of willing servants who will worship him to the end of eternity.

And Teddy arrived at Marymountain. Teddy frowned. The humans in Marymountain keep working so hard, they don't seem to be happy. Teddy wished to know why, and found a spot in Marymountain called the Crib to lay and observe these humans.

These humans seem to wear similar clothing, and do similar things. Teddy wanted to make them slaves, just as he did with other humans in the past. Strangely, the presence of Teddy in Marymountain calmed the humans.

They approached Teddy with more love than Teddy had ever experienced, praised and petted Teddy with the utmost passion, and most importantly, fed Teddy with the bestest food.

Pure ecstasy that the humans are subservient to Teddy again made Teddy want to help these humans get out of their misery. Teddy sympathized with their plight, and eventually learned of this devillish monster known as Grades.

Enraged, Teddy swore that this feline shall be the solution. With a flamboyant snicker, Teddy transformed into what these humans now call the almighty glorious entity, the Bell-Curve God.

And now, every single sufferer in Marymountain worships Teddy to their uttermost capability.
